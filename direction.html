EMBEDDED SYSTEM

Why this field?

With the rise of electric cars, automobiles are becoming increasingly intelligent. Currently, many cars have transitioned from physical buttons to touchscreens, with embedded systems at their core.

The Internet of Things (IoT) is a hot topic today, and smart homes and devices have become people's preferred choices. People are trying to control everything through a single screen. Embedded systems help people achieve this goal.

Precision measuring instruments display measurement data on the screen, and some use touch screens that allow people to control and adjust instrument parameters. The core of this lies in embedded systems.

Embedded systems are playing an increasingly important role in people's daily lives.
